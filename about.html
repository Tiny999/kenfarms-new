KenFarms is pivoting

Up until now, KenFarms have been focused on egg production. A great deal of success has been achieved in the line of business Employing the crowdfunding model for capital, with the help of investors, we were able to scale up our farm operations across multiple locations, with over 30-thousand-layer birds and 1, 800 crates of eggs harvested on a daily basis. However, moving forward, KenFarms is changing its focus as a business. While we still remain in the poultry business, we are pivoting from egg production to meat production.

KenFarms is repositioning to be a force in frozen chicken production in Nigeria, and we have laid bare our strategy and process in the detailed document that follows.
